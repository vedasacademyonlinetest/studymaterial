Theory — Circles & Theorems (వృత్తాలు)

Parts of Circle

Radius r: centre to edge. Diameter d=2r. Chord: both ends on circle. Arc: part of circumference. Sector: between two radii.

వ్యాసార్థం · జ్యా · చాపం · వృత్తఖండం

Angle in Semicircle

Angle subtended by diameter at any point on circle = 90° (Thales Theorem).

అర్ధవృత్తంలో కోణం = 90° — Thales

Central & Inscribed Angles

Inscribed angle = ½ × Central angle (same arc). Angles in same segment are equal.

కేంద్ర కోణం = 2 × పరివృత్త కోణం

Cyclic Quadrilateral

All 4 vertices on circle. Opposite angles supplementary (sum=180°). Exterior ∠ = opposite interior ∠.

చక్రీయ చతుర్భుజం · విరుద్ధ కోణాలు=180°

Tangent Properties

Tangent ⊥ Radius at contact. Two tangents from external point are equal (PA=PB).

స్పర్శరేఖ ⊥ వ్యాసార్థం | PA=PB

Chord Properties

Equal chords equidistant from centre. ⊥ from centre bisects chord. Longer chord closer to centre.

సమాన జ్యాలు · కేంద్రం నుండి సమదూరం

⭐ 7 Circle Theorems (IIT Foundation)

1. Central ∠ = 2 × Inscribed ∠ (same arc)
2. Angles in same segment = equal
3. Angle in semicircle = 90° (Thales)
4. Cyclic quad: opposite ∠s sum = 180°
5. Tangent ⊥ radius at contact
6. Two tangents from external point = equal
7. Alternate segment theorem: tangent-chord ∠ = inscribed ∠ in alternate segment

Solved Examples

Example 1 [Class 9–10] — Inscribed Angle

O is centre, ∠AOB=130°. Find ∠ACB (C on major arc).

  • 1Inscribed ∠ = ½ × central ∠ (theorem)
  • 2∠ACB = ½ × 130° = 65°
  • 3Any point on major arc gives same 65°
✓ ∠ACB = 65°
Example 2 [Class 9–10] — Cyclic Quad

ABCD cyclic. ∠A=3x, ∠C=2x+10. Find x.

  • 1∠A+∠C=180° (opposite angles in cyclic quad)
  • 23x+2x+10=180° → 5x=170° → x=34°
  • 3∠A=102°, ∠C=78°. Check: 102+78=180° ✓
✓ x=34°, ∠A=102°, ∠C=78°
Example 3 [Class 10–11] — Tangent Length

From P, tangents PA, PB to circle. r=5, OP=13. Find PA.

  • 1OA⊥PA (tangent-radius theorem)
  • 2PA² = OP²−OA² = 169−25 = 144 → PA=12
  • 3PA=PB=12cm (equal tangents). (5,12,13) triple!
✓ PA=PB=12cm | formula: √(d²−r²)

Theory — Coordinate Geometry (నిర్దేశాంక జ్యామితి)

Cartesian Plane

x-axis, y-axis, 4 quadrants. Point P(x,y). Origin O=(0,0). Q1:(+,+) Q2:(−,+) Q3:(−,−) Q4:(+,−)

కార్తీయ తలం · 4 చతుర్థాంశాలు

Distance Formula

d = √[(x₂−x₁)²+(y₂−y₁)²]
From origin: d=√(x²+y²)

దూర సూత్రం — Pythagoras నుండి

Section Formula

Dividing PQ in m:n (internal):
x=(mx₂+nx₁)/(m+n)
y=(my₂+ny₁)/(m+n)

విభజన సూత్రం — అంతర్గత

Midpoint

M=((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)
= Section formula m:n=1:1
Centroid=((Σx)/3,(Σy)/3)

మధ్యబిందు · కేంద్రకం

Slope & Lines

m=(y₂−y₁)/(x₂−x₁)=tanθ
Parallel: m₁=m₂
Perpendicular: m₁m₂=−1

వాలు · సమాంతర · లంబ రేఖలు

Area of Triangle

Δ=½|x₁(y₂−y₃)+x₂(y₃−y₁)+x₃(y₁−y₂)|
Area=0 ⟹ collinear points

నిర్దేశాంక వైశాల్యం (Shoelace)

⭐ Straight Line Forms (Class 10–11)

• Slope-intercept: y=mx+c
• Point-slope: y−y₁=m(x−x₁)
• Two-point: (y−y₁)/(y₂−y₁)=(x−x₁)/(x₂−x₁)
• Intercept form: x/a + y/b = 1
• Perpendicular distance: d=|ax₁+by₁+c|/√(a²+b²)

Solved Examples

Example 1 [Class 9–10] — Section Formula

Point dividing A(1,3) and B(7,9) in ratio 2:1.

  • 1m=2, n=1: x=(2×7+1×1)/3=15/3=5
  • 2y=(2×9+1×3)/3=21/3=7
✓ P(5,7)
Example 2 [Class 10] — Collinearity Test

Are A(1,1), B(4,4), C(7,7) collinear?

  • 1Area=½|1(4−7)+4(7−1)+7(1−4)|=½|−3+24−21|=0
  • 2Area=0 ⟹ collinear. All lie on y=x.
✓ Collinear — all on y=x
Example 3 [Class 11] — Distance from Line

Distance from P(3,4) to 3x+4y−15=0.

  • 1d=|3(3)+4(4)−15|/√(9+16)
  • 2d=|9+16−15|/5=10/5=2
✓ Distance = 2 units
