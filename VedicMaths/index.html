Sutra: Nikhilam (Base Method)
98 × 97 = ?
  • 1Base = 100. Deficiencies: 98→−2, 97→−3
  • 2Left part: 98−3 = 95 (or 97−2 = 95) ✓
  • 3Right part: (−2)×(−3) = 06 (2 digits for base 100)
  • 4Answer = 95 | 06
⚡ Final Answer 9506
Sutra: Ekadhikena Purvena (Squaring)
105² = ?
  • 1Base = 100. Surplus: 105→+5
  • 2Left part: 105 + 5 = 110
  • 3Right part: 5² = 25
  • 4Answer = 110 | 25
⚡ Final Answer 11025
Sutra: Urdhva-Tiryak (Cross Multiply)
23 × 41 = ?
  • 1Units × Units: 3×1 = 3 (write 3)
  • 2Cross: 2×1 + 3×4 = 2+12 = 14 (write 4, carry 1)
  • 3Tens × Tens: 2×4 = 8+1(carry) = 9
  • 4Read right to left: 9 | 4 | 3
⚡ Final Answer 943
